# Sajjad-ITDEV117-Individual-Project

Name of your application: SpltSmart
What problem the app solves: It calculates individual expenses, allows users to create custom payment plans, and maintains payment records to ensure organized financial tracking.
Who the app is for: College students who live together and friends who share costs for common expenses such as dining out, traveling, and paying bills.
The main features of the app: Users create a group, add participants, and enter expenses (including who paid and how much). 
A short explanation of how the app works: Friends enter shared expenses into SplitSmart, and the app calculates what each person owes and tracks completed payments.
