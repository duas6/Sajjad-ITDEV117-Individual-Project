# Sajjad-ITDEV117-Individual-Project

SpltSmart calculates individual expenses, allows users to create custom payment plans, and maintains payment records to ensure organized financial tracking. It is for College students who live together and friends who share costs for common expenses such as dining out, traveling, and paying bills. Users create a group, add participants, and enter expenses (including who paid and how much). Friends enter shared expenses into SplitSmart, and the app calculates what each person owes and tracks completed payments.
